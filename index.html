About Me


Hello there. I learned very early on in life that to be successful it takes a serious amount of hard work, being prepared for any eventuality, and just a dash of good luck. I seek to ensure that regardless of what I work on, it is fully functional and complete to any specification and will accept nothing less than that. When it comes to tackling new ideas and concepts, I excel at using previously obtained knowledge to not only understand what is going on, but to view them in unorthodox ways.

While I love science, math, and research, I am an avid fan of the arts. To me, nothing is more satisfying in life, then to start with a blank slate and create something that works or inspires others. I am a huge fan of music, art, and movies. I like to draw inspiration from the universe around me, as I create new projects.
